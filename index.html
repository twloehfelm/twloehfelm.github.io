What We Do

We provide feedback to radiologists when pathologic diagnoses are available for findings they've made, even if the pathology diagnosis occurs years after the radiology report. We believe that the best way to learn to be a better radiologist is to diligently review pathology outcomes and compare the path diagnosis with the radiology impression.

To accomplish this, we process free-text radiology and pathology reports through a custom natural language processing pipeline to identify the structure of the report and to extract anatomy and disease concepts. Our proprietary relevancy determination algorithm compares the annotations from each pathology report with every radiology report from the same patient. If the pathology report is determined to be relevant to the radiology report, then the reports are linked virtually. Any radiologist who participated in the generation of a relevant radiology report will be presented with the rad-path link in our clean and functional web interface, and can easily review the text of both the radiology and pathology reports side-by-side.
