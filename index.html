What We Do

Create software to address niche needs in healthcare, with a particular focus on data created by or useful to radiologists. We build solutions that are not available from larger medical software developers.

Why We Do It

Our mission is to improve the process of diagnosing disease by freeing information currently buried in the electronic medical record, making it accessible when and where it is most useful.

PATHFINDER

PATHFINDER® is a free-text analysis engine that extracts medical concepts from radiology and pathology reports.

The extracted data help determine relevancy between pairs of rad-path reports, and are used to build dashboards enabling radiologists to assess and improve their diagnostic accuracy.

The database of linked rad-path reports and extracted concepts support a range of education and research initiatives.
