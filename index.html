What We Do

Our goal is to make it easy for radiologists to learn from their own experience by automating the process of following-up on pathology results.

We monitor and analyze all radiology and pathology reports in your system, keep track of which radiologists generated which reports, and determine whether a given pathology report is relevant to each of the prior radiology reports from the same patient.

Radiologists can review these rad-path connections to assess and improve their own diagnostic abilities, even if the pathology diagnosis occurs years after the radiology report. We believe the best radiologists are continually learning from their own experience, and our mission is to facilitate that learning process.
security

Secure

Nothing leaves your network. The entire application and processing pipeline run locally, so your data stays in your systems. You have complete control over who can access the system.

person

Personal

We respect patient privacy. End-users only see cases where they have an established medical relationship with the patient by virtue of having already participated in the radiology or pathology report generation.

gps_fixed

Accurate

Our text processing pipeline and relevancy algorithm ensure that only high quality matches are shown. We review anatomy and disease terms found in specific sections of radiology and pathology reports, and leverage comprehensive biomedical knowledge bases to determine whether a given pathology report is relevant to a given radiology report.
