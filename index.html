Who We Are

We create software to address niche needs in healthcare, with a particular focus on data created by or useful to radiologists. We build solutions that are not available from larger medical software developers.

PANORAD PATH®

PATH® is a free-text analysis engine that extracts medical concepts from radiology and pathology reports.

The extracted data help determine relevancy between any pair of rad-path reports from the same patient, and are used to build dashboards enabling radiologists to assess and improve their own diagnostic performance.

The database of linked rad-path reports and the extracted concepts support diverse education and research missions.
